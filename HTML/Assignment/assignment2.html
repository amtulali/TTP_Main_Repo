Recipe 1: Pancakes

  • Ingredients
    • 1 cup of Flour
    • 2 tbsp sugar
    • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
    • Pinch of salt
    • 1 cup of milk
    • 2 tbsp of oil
    • 2 eggs
  • Instructions
    • Combine the dry ingredients.
    • Add the wet ingredients and mix.
    • Pour or ladle the batter onto the oiled griddle or pan.
    • Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook on the other side.
